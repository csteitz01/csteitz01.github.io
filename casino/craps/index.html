The Rules

Welcome to Simplified Craps!
The rules are as follows: You will be making bets on two dice being rolled, and your options for betting are called Pass or Don't Pass. Pass means you are betting a 7 or an 11 will be rolled first, while betting on Don't Pass means you are betting a 2, 3, or 12 will be rolled first. Any other numbers being rolled will result in rerolling until one of the previously stated numbers are rolled. Calling the bet correctly will result in doubling your bet, while calling it incorrectly will result in you losing that bet. You will be asked how much money you are bringing to the table, and you can't add more funds once the game has started. The Quit button will make sure you want to leave and take you back to the selection screen. The Roll Dice button rolls the dice, and the Rules button shows this screen again if you need a refresher.
